Writing for Professional Registration

The Professional Review Report is the most critical document in your application for CEng or IEng. Unlike a standard academic thesis or technical whitepaper, its primary purpose is not to explain how technology works, but to prove how you work.

Competence vs. Technical Description

Reviewers are looking for evidence of your personal contribution. A common mistake is describing the "Team's" achievements ("We designed a bridge...") rather than your specific role ("I calculated the wind loading...").

  • The Technical Report (Option for some routes): Demonstrates deep technical knowledge (UK-SPEC A) equivalent to Masters level (for CEng) or Bachelors level (for IEng).
  • The Competence Report: Maps your career history against the 5 UK-SPEC standards (A-E), proving you are a safe, competent, and ethical professional.
