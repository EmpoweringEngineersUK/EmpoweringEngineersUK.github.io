Welcome to the Engineering Competence Framework. This platform is designed to reverse-engineer the UK-SPEC requirements, helping you build a professional registration report (CEng/IEng) from the ground up.

1. The Core Philosophy

Engineering registration is not a test of technical memory; it is a test of competence. The reviewers do not just want to know what you built; they want to know how you managed the risk, budget, safety, and ethics of building it.

2. Recommended Workflow

  • Step 1 - CPD Gap Analysis: Use the Gap Analyzer first. Be honest with the sliders. This generates your "Hit List" of missing evidence.
  • Step 2 - Draft Evidence: Take your strongest project and run it through the STAR Engine. This forces you to be concise.
  • Step 3 - Audit: Copy your STAR text into the Reviewer Tool. It will strip out passive language ("we decided") and force you to use active language ("I authorized").
  • Step 4 - Ethics: Use the Ethics Compass to prepare for the "Dilemma Question" in your interview.

3. Privacy Protocol

This site uses "Local Storage" technology. When you type into the STAR Engine or CPD tool, the data is saved on your own device. We never see your drafts. This ensures you can write confidentially about sensitive projects.

We encourage the use of AI (like our "Mega-Prompts") to structure your thinking and critique your writing. However, you must never use AI to fabricate evidence.

Professional Registration is a declaration of your competence. Using AI to generate examples you did not experience is a breach of the Code of Conduct. Use these tools as a "Co-Pilot," not an "Autopilot."
