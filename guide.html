>> SYSTEM_OVERVIEW

Welcome to the Engineering Competence Framework. This platform is designed to reverse-engineer the UK-SPEC requirements, helping you build a professional registration report (CEng/IEng) from the ground up.

Goal: Transform your raw engineering experience into structured, evidence-based competency statements.

>> STEP 1: GENERATE RAW EVIDENCE

Tool: STAR Engine

Most engineers fail because they focus on "We" (the team) instead of "I" (the engineer). The STAR Engine forces you to break this habit.

Procedure:

  • Situation: Briefly describe the problem (1-2 sentences). Don't waffle.
  • Task: What was specifically YOUR responsibility?
  • Action: The most important part. What technical calculations, decisions, or leadership steps did YOU take?
  • Result: What was the outcome? Did you save money, improve safety, or meet a deadline?
PRO TIP: Don't worry about word count yet. Just get the facts down. The tool will assemble them into a coherent paragraph for you.
Launch STAR Engine

>> STEP 2: AUDIT & REFINE

Tool: STAR Reviewer

Once you have a draft paragraph, you need to know if it actually hits the UK-SPEC criteria. Is it strong enough for CEng?

Procedure:

  • Paste your generated STAR paragraph into the Reviewer.
  • Analyze: The tool will scan for "Weak Language" (e.g., "I helped", "I supported") and suggest "Strong Language" (e.g., "I formulated", "I authorized").
  • Keyword Check: It checks if you have included mandatory buzzwords like "Safety", "Budget", "Sustainability", and "Ethics".
Launch Reviewer

>> STEP 3: FILL THE GAPS

Tools: Ethics Compass & CPD Analyzer

Technical evidence is easy. The "soft skills" (Ethics, Leadership, CPD) are where most applications fail.

Procedure:

  • Ethics: Use the Ethics Compass to turn a generic "I worked safely" statement into a specific reference to the RAEng Statement of Ethical Principles.
  • CPD: Use the Gap Analyzer to identify which UK-SPEC competencies (A-E) you are missing evidence for, and generate a plan to fix it.
