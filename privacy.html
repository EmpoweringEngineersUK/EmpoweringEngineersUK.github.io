At Empowering Engineers UK, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data in compliance with the UK GDPR and Google's policies.

1. Data We Collect

We do not require you to create an account to use our tools. Most data (such as the text you type into the STAR Engine) is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., which pages are most popular). This data is anonymized.
  • Cookies: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

2. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Local Storage

Our tools (STAR Engine, Ethics Compass, etc.) use your browser's "Local Storage" to save your progress (Auto-Save). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Reset" buttons within the tools.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data we hold. Since we do not store personal accounts, simply clearing your cookies effectively removes your interaction history with us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: info@empoweringengineers.uk
