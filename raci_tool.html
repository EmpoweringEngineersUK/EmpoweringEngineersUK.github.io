Project Responsibility Matrix

Generate, edit, and download RACI matrices for engineering scenarios. Demonstrating clear accountability is key for UK-SPEC Competencies C, D, and E. Progress is saved automatically to your browser.

R (Responsible)
The "Doer". The engineer performing the calculation, design, or activity.
A (Accountable)
The "Owner". The person signing off (e.g., Lead Engineer/Chair).
C (Consulted)
Two-way info. Experts whose input is required before proceeding.
I (Informed)
One-way info. Stakeholders updated after the fact.

How to use this for Chartership

Competency C (Technical & Commercial Leadership): Use the Technical or NPD templates to show you understand who is Accountable (A) vs who does the work (R).
Competency D (Communication): The Seminar or Lunch & Learn templates demonstrate you can coordinate diverse stakeholders.
Competency E (Professional Commitment): Use the STEM or CSR templates to evidence your volunteering leadership in a structured, professional format.
